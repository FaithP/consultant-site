About Me

With over 15 years of experience spanning software development, healthcare systems, educational technology, and business operations, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My background in both technical implementation and strategic planning allows me to bridge the gap between vision and execution.

I believe in a human-centered approach to problem-solving, focusing on the people affected by systems and processes rather than just the technology or business metrics. This philosophy has guided my work with organizations of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies.
